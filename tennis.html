Data Exploration - ATP Tennis

SQL JupyterNB

Base for the analysis was a csv from kaggle.com with more than 60k ATP matches ranging from 2000 until 2023. I created a SQLite database and imported the csv. All the querying then was done in SQL on Jupyter Notebook for better visualization.

The following questions were asked and answered:

  • How many tournaments are on the ATP Tour?
  • Who were the Top 10 of players who won the most matches in the last 24 years and how many did they win?
  • Which player won the most matches in a single year, when was it and how many matches were won?
  • Who were the Top 10 of players who won the most tournaments in the last 24 years and how many did they win?
  • Who won the most tournaments on Grass, Hard Court and Clay
  • Who were the Top 10 of players who won tournaments this year (2023) and how many?
  • How many duels were fought between Rafael Nadal and Roger Federer and who won?
    • Whats the head to head statistic between them?
