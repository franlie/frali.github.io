Property Dashboard

Tableau Listly Excel

A dashboard for visualizing property data in the area of nuremberg on August 23. The data was scraped from immoscout using listly based on a search for buying appartments in nuremberg (area of 30km). Data cleaning and structuring was done in Excel due to the relatively small dataset (roughly 1.000 appartments). The Dashboard consists of Histograms for prices, number of rooms, and square meters. Additionally i added a ranking for commercial providers grouped by the price sum of all the properties they are offering. Finally the average price per square meter gets calculated and refreshed referring to the number of rooms. Unfortunately the construction year didnt get scraped successfully.
